## Main points & what's current vs. outdated

**Core thesis of the deck:** HK banks are not short of funds, yet 40%+ of SME loan applications are still delayed, cut, or rejected — usually because of (1) messy financial statements, (2) applying "blind" to the wrong bank, and (3) the time drain of preparing documents. The proposed remedy is to clean up the accounts, match the applicant to the right bank *tier*, and structure the application for the maximum eligible limit before submitting.

**Still accurate for 2026:**
- **SFGS 80% guarantee is the current mainstay** — max **HK$18M**, repayment up to **10 years**, and the scheme has been **extended to 31 March 2028**.
- **Bank-tier behaviour holds:** Tier-1 traditional banks (HSBC / BOC / Hang Seng) remain strict (perfect audited accounts, 4–8 weeks); Tier-2 commercial banks (DBS / BEA / Dah Sing) accept good management accounts (3–6 weeks); virtual banks (ZA Bank / PAObank / livi) are the most flexible, leaning on real-time bank-statement data (1–2 weeks).
- **Data-driven / alternative lending** (POS & credit-card settlement data, no audited report) is now a genuine mainstream path for startups and retail/F&B.

**Outdated / needs a freshness flag:**
- The **90% and 100% guarantee schemes have ended** — the deck itself notes the market's fund flows have shifted because of this. Any current guidance should lead with SFGS 80% (to Mar 2028), not the retired 90%/100% products.
- The specific loan-size bands (e.g. HK$1–3M unsecured) are indicative and move with lender appetite — treat as ballpark, not fixed.

## Page 2 — The problem: banks have money, why is SME financing still hard?

**中文** — 銀行資金充裕，為何中小企融資仍困難重重？
- **40%+** 中小企貸款申請面臨延遲、減額甚至被拒絕。現時香港銀行不缺資金，但融資困難依然存在。
- **財務報表混亂** — 未平帳目及現金流呈現欠佳，令銀行的信貸部門卻步。→ 信貸評級受損風險
- **盲目申請** — 每間銀行對不同行業有「隱藏偏好」。申錯銀行必遭拒絕，更會影響商業信貸評級。→ 申請記錄影響評分
- **耗費時間** — 創辦人花費數週時間處理繁瑣文件，無暇管理核心業務。→ 核心業務被耽誤

**English** — Banks are flush with capital, so why is SME financing still so difficult?
- **40%+** of SME loan applications face delay, reduction, or rejection. HK banks are not short of funds, but financing difficulty persists.
- **Messy financial statements** — unbalanced accounts and weak cash-flow presentation make the bank's credit department back off. → Risk of a damaged credit rating.
- **Blind applications** — each bank has "hidden preferences" by industry. Apply to the wrong bank and you'll be rejected — and it dents your commercial credit score. → Application record affects your scoring.
- **Time drain** — founders spend weeks on tedious paperwork with no time left for the core business. → Core business gets neglected.

## Page 3 — The edge: tailor-made, approval-ready packaging

**中文** — 度身打造獲批方案（專業會計團隊，在銀行審批前修復所有基礎問題）
- **財務重整 (Financial Cleanup)** — 重組及包裝管理帳目、強積金紀錄及銀行流水，完美迎合銀行審批的嚴格要求。→ 帳目合規化
- **精準配對銀行 (Precision Matching)** — 掌握各銀行（一線大行、二線商業銀行或虛擬銀行）最新行業偏好，一擊即中。→ 命中率最大化
- **爭取最高額度 (Maximizing the Limit)** — 根據資產及營收歷史，為您爭取絕對最高貸款額，而非銀行給予的最低限額。→ 貸款額極大化

**English** — Tailor-made, approval-ready solutions (a professional accounting team fixes every foundational issue before the bank reviews)
- **Financial Cleanup** — reorganize and package the management accounts, MPF records, and bank statements to meet banks' strict approval requirements. → Compliant books.
- **Precision Matching** — know each bank's latest industry preferences (tier-1 majors, tier-2 commercial, or virtual banks) and hit the target first time. → Maximized hit rate.
- **Maximizing the Limit** — based on assets and revenue history, push for the absolute highest loan amount rather than the bank's lowest offer. → Maximized loan size.
